#Seek #Tagger

  1. Enter a CSV of tags below and click "Save".
  2. On seek.co.nz, type in a search query.
  3. The extension will scan job listings for matches with your tags.
  4. Matched tags will be displayed directly on the search results page.

Repo: github.com/indiekiwi/chrome-ext-seek-tagger

This plugin is NOT associated with seek.co.nz
